Features

  • Resort Booking: Users can browse and book various resorts in Dandeli, a popular tourist destination in Karnataka, India.
  • Resort Reviews: Users can read and write reviews about the resorts they have visited or booked, and rate them on various criteria, such as cleanliness, service, location, etc.
